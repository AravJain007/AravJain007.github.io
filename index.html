My Projects

Narad

Jan 2024

  • Dynamic Query Resolution System: Conceptualized and executed a dynamic query resolution system, integrating cutting-edge technologies, such as AI4Bharat ASR & Translational model, FAISS vector database, Gemini API, and HuggingFace models.
  • Scalable Product: Engineered a scalable product supporting text conversations across all 22 major languages of India. The system operates at three speeds: superfast providing responses in 40ms using semantic search, and fast/normal modes utilizing semantic search and Language Models for context-aware responses.
  • Efficient GPU Utilization: Performed quantization on all models and integrated them onto a singular P100 GPU for streamlined and efficient GPU utilization.
  • Personalization and Recommendations: Personalization provided by recommendations using Contextual Bandits, Collaborative Filtering, Machine Learning, and geo-specific recommendations. A BERT and Keras based model was used to make a multi-class text and intention classifier to aid the recommendation system.
  • Fine Tuning and RLHF: Fine-tuning and RLHF were done on custom-created Bhuvan datasets using Transformer Reinforcement Library by HuggingFace.
